\section*{UOps}

All nodes in the tinygrad graph are \textbf{UOps}. A UOp is a triple $(\mathrm{op},\;\mathrm{src},\;\mathrm{arg})$ where $\mathrm{op}$ is from the set below, $\mathrm{src}$ is a tuple of input UOps, and $\mathrm{arg}$ is op-dependent. The full program is a DAG of UOps. Each UOp has two derived properties, \textbf{dtype} and \textbf{shape}, determined by the rules at the end of this document.

%% ============================================================
\subsection*{Source Ops \normalfont\small--- leaf nodes}

\begin{tabular}{@{}l l l l@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Op} & \textbf{src} & \textbf{arg} & \textbf{Semantics} \\
\midrule
\op{Buffer} & () & size, dtype &
  A flat allocation of memory with shape $(\textit{size},)$. \\[4pt]
\op{Const} & () & value, dtype &
  A scalar constant with shape $(\ )$. \\[4pt]
\op{Param} & $(\mathbf{s},)$ & slot, dtype & Bound variable with shape $\mathbf{s}$. Replaced by $a_{\text{slot}}$ in \op{Call}. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

%% ============================================================
\subsection*{{\color{movgreen}Movement Ops} \normalfont\small--- no arithmetic, shapes are \op{Vectorize} UOps in src}

\begin{tabular}{@{}l l l l@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Op} & \textbf{src} & \textbf{arg} & \textbf{Semantics} \\
\midrule
\op{Reshape} & $(T, \mathbf{s'})$ & --- & Reinterpret in row-major order. $\prod s_k = \prod s'_k$. \\
\op{Expand} & $(T, \mathbf{s'})$ & --- & Broadcast size-1 axes. $s_k \in \{1, s'_k\}$. \\
\op{Pad} & $(T, \mathbf{s'})$ & --- & Pad right side with $0$s to a larger shape. \\
\op{Shrink} & $(T, \mathbf{s'})$ & --- & Shrink from the right side. Inverse of \op{Pad}. \\
\op{Permute} & $(T,)$ & axis order $\pi$ & Reorder axes. $\pi = (1,0)$ is transpose. \\
\op{Flip} & $(T,)$ & bools $\mathbf{f}$ & Reverse along flagged axes. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

%% ============================================================
\subsection*{{\color{elwyellow}Elementwise Ops} \normalfont\small--- all inputs same shape, output same shape, applied per-element}

\begin{tabular}{@{}l l l l@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Arity} & \textbf{src} & \textbf{Op} & \textbf{Semantics} \\
\midrule
Unary & $(T,)$
  & \op{Exp2}, \op{Log2}, \op{Sin}, \op{Sqrt}, \op{Neg}, \op{Recip}
  & $2^x$, $\log_2 x$, $\sin x$, $\sqrt{x}$, $-x$, $1/x$ \\
& & \op{Cast}
  & Convert to target dtype (specified in arg). \\[4pt]
Binary & $(A, B)$
  & \op{Add}, \op{Mul}, \op{Max}, \op{Mod}, \op{Idiv}
  & $a+b$, $a \cdot b$, $\max(a,b)$, $a \bmod b$, $\lfloor a/b \rfloor$ \\
& & \op{CmpLt}, \op{CmpNe}
  & $[a < b]$, $[a \ne b]$ \\
& & \op{Xor}, \op{Or}, \op{And}, \op{Shr}, \op{Shl}
  & $a \oplus b$, $a \mid b$, $a \mathbin{\&} b$, $a \gg b$, $a \ll b$ \\[4pt]
Ternary & $(P, A, B)$
  & \op{Where}
  & $A[\mathbf{i}]$ if $P[\mathbf{i}] \ne 0$, else $B[\mathbf{i}]$ \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

\smallskip
Sub is \op{Add}+\op{Neg}. Div is \op{Mul}+\op{Recip}. Greater-than is \op{CmpLt} with swapped operands.

%% ============================================================
\subsection*{{\color{callblue}Call Ops} \normalfont\small--- function abstraction, like the lambda calculus}

\begin{tabular}{@{}l l l l@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Op} & \textbf{src} & \textbf{arg} & \textbf{Semantics} \\
\midrule
\op{Call} & (body, $a_0$, $a_1$, \ldots) & --- & Substitute each \op{Param} $k$ in body with $a_k$. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

%% ============================================================
\subsection*{Other Ops}

\begin{tabular}{@{}l l l l@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Op} & \textbf{src} & \textbf{arg} & \textbf{Semantics} \\
\midrule
\op{Vectorize} & $(x_0, x_1, \ldots)$ & --- & Group scalars into a tuple. Used to represent shapes. \\
\op{Range} & $(\text{bound},)$ & --- & Iterator from $0$ to bound. \\
\op{Index} & (buf, $i_0$, $i_1$, \ldots) & --- & Index from the left: $k$ indices consume $k$ leading dims. \\
\op{Store} & (buf, val) & --- & Store val into buf. \\
\op{After} & (val, deps\ldots) & --- & Passthrough of val; guarantees deps execute first. \\
\op{Linear} & (uops\ldots) & --- & Linearized (toposorted) instruction sequence for a kernel. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

%% ============================================================
\subsection*{Derived Properties}

Every UOp has a \textbf{dtype} and \textbf{shape}, derived from its op, src, and arg:

\medskip
\begin{tabular}{@{}l l l@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Op} & \textbf{dtype} & \textbf{shape} \\
\midrule
\op{Buffer} & from arg & $(\text{size},)$ from arg \\
\op{Const} & from arg & $()$ \\
\op{Param} & from arg & from $\mathrm{src}[0]$ \\[3pt]
\op{Reshape}, \op{Expand}, \op{Pad}, \op{Shrink} & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{dtype}$ & from $\mathrm{src}[1]$ \\
\op{Permute} & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{dtype}$ & permute $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{shape}$ by $\pi$ \\
\op{Flip} & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{dtype}$ & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{shape}$ \\[3pt]
\op{Cast} & from arg & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{shape}$ \\
ALU unary & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{dtype}$ & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{shape}$ \\
ALU binary & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{dtype}$ & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{shape}$ \\
\op{CmpLt}, \op{CmpNe} & bool & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{shape}$ \\
\op{Where} & $\mathrm{src}[1].\mathrm{dtype}$ & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{shape}$ \\[3pt]
\op{Call} & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{dtype}$ & substitute \op{Param} shapes \\
\op{Index} & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{dtype}$ & remaining: $(s_k, \ldots, s_{n-1})$ \\
\op{Store} & void & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{shape}$ \\
\op{After} & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{dtype}$ & $\mathrm{src}[0].\mathrm{shape}$ \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
